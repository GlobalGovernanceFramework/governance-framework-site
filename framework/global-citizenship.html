Global Citizenship Framework

A framework for understanding and practicing global citizenship

This framework provides a structured pathway for individuals and communities to develop from awareness to active participation in our interconnected world.

What is Global Citizenship?

Global citizenship is not a legal status—it's a way of being. It means seeing yourself as part of one human family, responsible for the wellbeing of all people, species, and ecosystems on Earth—and beyond.

It expands our identity beyond borders, fosters shared responsibility, and connects local action to global impact.

❤️

⚖️

🔍

🕊️

🌱

🤝

Resolve conflict through listening and cooperation

🔷

🙏

5 Levels of Practice

Global citizenship develops through these progressive levels:

1. Awareness

"I'm learning about our world"

Learn about global systems and issues

2. Understanding

"I'm thinking systemically"

Analyze root causes and interconnections

3. Empathy & Identity

"We're all in this together"

Feel connected to global communities

4. Participation

"I make choices that help the world"

Take action to address global challenges

5. Leadership

"I'm helping humanity flourish"

Build systems and inspire others

Tangible Benefits

For Individuals

For Communities

For the Planet
