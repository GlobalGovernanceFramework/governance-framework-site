1

Map & Categorize

Brainstorm all relevant stakeholders. Place each in appropriate quadrant. Include internal staff, leadership, board members, key external partners, funders, beneficiaries.

2

Assess Influence

For each stakeholder, rate their influence on regeneration success (1-5 scale). Mark high-influence stakeholders with a star or highlight.

3

Strategy Planning

Develop specific engagement strategies: How to activate champions? Address blocker concerns? Move fence-sitters? Leverage informal power?
